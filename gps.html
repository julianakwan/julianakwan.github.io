Emulation and Gaussian Processes

Connecting models to observations is one of the important functions of a scientist. Unfortunately, cosmological N-body simulations can be incredibly expensive, often taking millions of CPU hours. In this section, we introduce a technique called Gaussian Process regression that can learn the non-linear relationships between the cosmological variables to make predictions that are both fast and accurate.
